v=final velocity
u=initial Velocity

t is the time taken
x = u*t*cosθ, y = (u*t*sinθ)−((g*t)/2)

Horizontal range(R)=(u*u*sin2θ)/g
Max Height(H)=(u*u*sinθ*sinθ)/2*g
